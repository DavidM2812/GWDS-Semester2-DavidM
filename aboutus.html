About Us

Go! Bike's mission is simple; provide a better, healthier and greener alternative to automovotive transportation.

Littered across the city centre of Bristol, we have set up stations where you can link your phone to our bikes, and use them to your leisure. Whether this is for commuting to work, getting to hangout spots, or just explorig the city, or even performing mundane tasks like getting groceries! Our bikes come in all shapes and sizes, for children, for adults, for shoppers or for sports. Go! Bike believes in promoting good health, and imrpoving the quality of air and reducing the noise around Bristol, which is why we wholeheartidly encourage making the switch, and going green. Go! Bikes service not only provide an alternative method of travelling, but also a hassle free mode of traveling. With a single payment from your mobile phone, your account is now linked to our bikes, which you are free to use at your leisure.
